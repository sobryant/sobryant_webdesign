Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Starbucks Cinnamon Dolce Syrup
  • 8 oz hot apple cider
  • sweetened whipped cream
  • caramel sauce

Instructions

  2. Add hot cider.
  3. Top with whipped cream and drizzle with caramel sauce
