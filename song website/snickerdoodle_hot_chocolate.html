Snickerdoodle Hot Chocolate

home next

Ingredients

  • 3 c. half and half (can substitute with fat free half and half or lowfat milk)
  • 2/3 c. white chocolate chopped
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp. nutmeg

The rain falls to the earth

  • The rain falls to the earth
  • And dances in puddles
  • One drop always next to another
  • The rain falls to the earth
  • Together with friends

Instructions

  1. Combine 1/4 c. half and half, white chocolate, and cinnamon stick in a small saucepan.
  2. Heat on low, stirring constantly, until white chocolate is melted.
  3. Add remaining half and half and spices and heat through.
  4. Remove cinnamon stick before serving.
