Easy Blender Eggnog

home beginning

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¾ cup brandy
  • 8 teaspoons sugar or more, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the heavy cream, sugar, and nutmeg just to a simme
  2. In a blender, blend the eggs on low speed for 1 minute. With the motor running, gradually add the hot cream mixture. Blend until frothy, about 30 seconds more. Add the brandy and blend to combine. Serve warm.
